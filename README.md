# The 2026 Low-Ticket Funnel Playbook

A tactical low-ticket funnel operating manual for SaaS, professional services, and local businesses in 2026. The math first (because most low-ticket offers fail on math, not creative). The 7-step funnel architecture from lead magnet to backend. Page-by-page specs with copy templates and word counts. Vertical plays for SaaS trials, paid intake for lawyers and dentists, and local first-visit voucher funnels. The 7-day buyer email sequence. FTC Click-to-Cancel and Stripe chargeback rules. The pricing psychology that still works in 2026.

## What's Inside

- Funnel math worked through: AOV needed to cover paid CAC, the order-bump and OTO contribution to AOV, the LTV math that makes a -$10 first sale profitable inside 90 days.
- The 7-step 2026 funnel architecture: lead magnet to tripwire to bump to OTO to downsell to continuity to backend. With dollar amounts at each step and the math floor.
- Page-by-page specs: squeeze, sales, order, OTO, downsell, thank-you. Conversion benchmarks, word counts, layout rules, and copy templates for each.
- Vertical plays: SaaS $1 paid trial, lifetime deal launch tactics, annual upfront. Professional services paid intake. Local first-visit voucher funnels.
- The 7-day buyer email sequence with subject lines and body templates. Plus the 30/60/90 expansion path that converts low-ticket buyers to high-ticket clients.
- Tracking + compliance: iOS attribution post-Jan 2026, FTC Click-to-Cancel rule status, Stripe chargeback rules with the $15 + $15 fee math, and continuity offer copy that survives dispute monitoring.
- Pricing psychology that still works in 2026: the $7, $27, $47, $97 thresholds, charm pricing vs round numbers, and why the $1 trial pattern works when nothing else does.
- The 10 most common 2026 failures and the fix for each. From under-priced OTOs to fulfillment friction to over-stacked Hormozi-style scammy stacks.
